Mcdonald market segmentation analysis

Mcdonald is the world’s largest fast food restaurant chain, serving over 69 millon customers daily in over 100 countries. Mcdonald’s is best known for its hamburgers, cheeseburgers and French fries, although their menu also includes other items like chicken, fish, fruit and salads. This analysis will help mcdonald’s to better understand their customers and tailor their products and marketing strategies accordingly. This analysis was done using powerbi.
